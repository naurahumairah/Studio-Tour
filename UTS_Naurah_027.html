About Us

Experience Your Own Magical World

Step into a magical world at the Harry Potter Studio Tour, exploring iconic sets, authentic props, and enchanting behind-the-scenes experiences.

Experience Your Own Magical World with the Harry Potter Studio Tour, where fans can step into the enchanting universe of the beloved film series. Hosted at Warner Bros. Studio in London, this interactive experience offers visitors a unique opportunity to explore iconic sets like the Great Hall and Diagon Alley, view original costumes and props, and learn about the spellbinding special effects and animatronics used in the movies. From seasonal events like Hogwarts in the Snow to exclusive dining experiences in the Great Hall, the tour brings the magic to life for fans of all age.

While we’re always discovering new magic, trust us when we say our Studio Tour reveals secrets more captivating than a Pensieve’s memory.
